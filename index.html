Public / Private Sector

I am fascinated by the power of the government. Lately I'm also mesmerized by the power of the private sector, and specifically Silicon Valley.

Can you image what the two can do, if they get together in a sensible way that protects the public interest, but empowers the private sector as well.

Currently I am almost two months into a Presidential Innovation Fellow, where I am working for the federal government as a G-14 employee, fresh from the private sector.

As expected, I hit the thick, beuacratic sludge of the federal government that I expected, and while I'm pushing forward and finding a way to deal, I'm struggling with a pretty immovable obstacle--the current federal government shutdown.

As I do with every aspect of my existence, I setup a domain, a blog, and begin to work through my thoughts. This represents my thoughts on what truly is possible with a public / private sector partnership.

Right now this site will reflect my own thoughts coming from the private sector and being placed within the federal government, but it will quickly evolve into what public / private sector partnerships can truly look like.

This site will change daily, if the government shutdown goes on longer than expected, it might change much quicker.
